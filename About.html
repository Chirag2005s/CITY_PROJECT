About Sirohi City

The Land of Temples, Culture & Aravalli Hills

Sirohi is a historic city located in the southern part of Rajasthan, surrounded by the beautiful Aravalli mountain range. Known for its calm environment, spiritual places, and cultural heritage, Sirohi is often referred to as the gateway to Mount Abu.

Did you know?
Sirohi was once the capital of the princely state of Sirohi and has deep historical roots dating back several centuries.

Historical Importance

The city has a rich history influenced by Rajput rulers and Jain culture. Ancient temples, forts, and monuments reflect the architectural brilliance of earlier times. Sirohi played an important role during the princely era of Rajasthan.

Temples & Spirituality

Sirohi is famous for its religious harmony. Major temples include Sarneshwar Mahadev Temple, Sundha Mata Temple, and various Jain temples. These places attract devotees and tourists from different parts of India.

Culture & Lifestyle

The culture of Sirohi reflects traditional Rajasthani values. Folk music, festivals, colorful fairs, and local handicrafts showcase the vibrant lifestyle of the people living here.

Geography & Climate

Situated near the Aravalli hills, Sirohi enjoys a pleasant climate compared to many other regions of Rajasthan. The surrounding greenery and hills add natural beauty to the city.
